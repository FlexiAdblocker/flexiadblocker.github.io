How to add our Adblocker Extension
Step 01

Click the Add Button

Scroll up and click on “Add to Chrome” or “Add to Edge” — depending on your browser.

Step 02

Redirect to Store

You’ll be taken to the official extension store page in a new tab (Chrome Web Store or Edge Add-ons).

Step 03

Add the Extension

Click “Add to Chrome” or “Get” on the store page. Then confirm by clicking “Add Extension” in the popup.

Step 04

Done!

The extension installs instantly. Look for the FlexiAdBlocker icon in your browser toolbar — you’re now protected from ads and trackers!

What is FlexiAdBlocker?

FlexiAdBlocker is a browser extension that blocks intrusive ads, pop-ups, and trackers — giving you a cleaner, faster, and safer browsing experience.

Is FlexiAdBlocker free to use?

Yes! FlexiAdBlocker is completely free to install and use. There are no hidden charges or subscriptions.

Which browsers are supported?

Currently, FlexiAdBlocker supports Firefox, Safari and Microsoft Edge. Support for more browsers will be added soon.

How can I turn ad blocking on or off?

Once installed, click the FlexiAdBlocker icon in your browser toolbar. You can toggle the ad blocking on or off instantly.

Will this extension slow down my browser?

Not at all. FlexiAdBlocker is lightweight and optimized for performance, helping web pages load faster by removing unnecessary ad content.
